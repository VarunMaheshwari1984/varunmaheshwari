About Me

I am passionate about creating high performing teams and organisations. I'm most attracted to solving real customer problems while generating business benefits.

I specialise in solving problems like :

  • Lack of Commitment and Ownership in the team.
  • People speaking different languages across the organisation.
  • Our priorities change quiet frequently.
  • We struggle to get balance between dev and ops work.
  • We are doing scrum perfectly but still not getting better.
  • We are good at commiting but not at Completing.
  • There are silos within our scrum team.
  • We struggle to slice work in vertical slices of Value.
  • We deliver user stories but not business results.
